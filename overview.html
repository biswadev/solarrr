OVERVIEW
Introduction

Global Knowledge Park will act as the single point solution - fulfilling need of high quality education through English medium right from primary level up to degree and professional courses level. This will effectively eliminate the need for going out of Deoband for anyone-least all for the Muslims girls who have to forget about higher education at present because their families can't or donít want to send them away.


All important courses - U.G/P.G. programmes, Business Management, Education, Medical/Paramedical/Nursing, Journalism and Mass Communication, Professional courses for women are envisaged - each of them housed in their separate department in Global Knowledge Park Campus. Sports Academy will be an added feature fulfilling the need of such facilities for students from school to all department of Global Knowledge Park. There would be a facility preparing our students for civil services entrance exams under the same roof. Global Knowledge Park will also help those youths to enter into main stream education who aspire to secure good jobs under Central and State Governments and will generate jobs for the region. Established of Global Knowledge Park in Deoband will also have a salutary effect by encouraging exchange of ideas and opening doors wide to modern education with the students of madrasas.

Why Deoband?

However important Deoband might be in realm of Islamic education because of Darul Uloom there is a huge demand for an education institute that would cater to the needs of students aspiring for quality education at primary, secondary and higher level. Presently, in absence of any such quality institute, the students have to move out of Deoband for undertaking quality schooling and higher education.


More than 3 lakh inhabitants in Deoband and nearby villages are mostly middle class families dependent largely depending on agricultural income and thus, cannot afford to send their children to Noida, Delhi, Chandigarh, Dehradun etc., for high quality education. Add to this the reservation conservative families have about sending their daughters outside Deoband for education.
