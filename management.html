People Behind the Project

Late Mr.Faizan-ul-haq
Founder

Best known as the harbinger of "SPRING DALE PUBLIC SCHOOL., DEOBAND", MADINA EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY" is the brain-child of Mr. Faizal-ul-Haq Siddiqui whose ideas are being converted into reality by the Madina Educational Society. The Founder Mr. Faizan-ul-Haq Siddiqui, an engineer from A.M.U by education, had conceived and nurtured the idea of Madina Educational Society for the benefit of his community back home in Deoband, while serving at King Abdul Aziz University, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia for 22 years. On the merited recommendation of late Sheikh Abdul Hasan Ali Nadwi (Ali Miyan) President Muslim Personal Law Board to Mr. Faizan-ul-Haq Madina Educational Society was formed in 1995. The first offering of Madina Educational Society came 20 years ago in the form of Spring Dale Public School. Taking off in a rather modest manner, the SPRING DALE PUBLIC SCHOOL is regarded as the finest institution for education for the children in this region.

Mr.saad siddiqui

Chairman

Born and brought up in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, Mr. Saad Siddiqui did his Schooling at Jeddah and then studied at Roorkee. A Commerce Graduation from CCS University, Mr. Saad Siddiqui did post graduate diploma in management from NIS and perused MBA. Having excellent track record of managing various corporate entities in Dubai till 2004 and then successfully running factories in Noida and Delhi, he became more and more interested in the field of education. Mr. Saad Siddiqui has widely traveled and interacted with different cross-sections of the society. This close and sustained social interaction with large number of people has caused him to dream of the Global Knowledge Park. To quote Mr. Saad Siddiqui - "It is extremely important to understand that the restlessness witnessed among the youth needs to be given proper direction else the enthusiasm without direction can bring disasters. They need to be counseled, must be trained to become corporate-ready and should be technically skilled through job-oriented courses so that their dreams can be given a flight."

Mr.Ahmed F.siddiqui

Joint Chairman

Mr. Ahmad Siddiqui is Biotech Engineer by profession having education at Embassy of India School and SGRR School, Dehradun. After completion of his education, he joined hands with Spring Dale Public School - a flagship product of Madina Educational Society. Blessed with creativity, zeal, enthusiasm and idealism which he seem to have inherited from his illustrious parents. Mr. Ahmed has now devoted his boundless energy in realizing the dream of Global Knowledge Park. The steady growth and success of Spring Dales School is largely due to the untiring work and missionary zeal exhibited by Mr. Ahmed Siddique in the capacity of school's Manager.
