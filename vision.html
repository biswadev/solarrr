VISION & MISSION

Vision

  • Imparting high quality main stream education through English Medium at School level/UG/PG/professional courses/technical education.
  • Preparing the students at various levels for a highly successful career through whatever path they prefer to build it.
  • Encouraging Sports at all levels and age groups.
  • Gradually converting the Global Knowledge Park into a full-fledged university.

Mission

One window solutions for all your education needs.

Global Knowledge Park would accommodate in its various wings prime institutions - each of which would cater to different needs of the society. It would be a single stop solution - having a Management College, a women Degree College, a Paramedical College, a Sports Academy. At the core of it would be Spring Dale Public School providing education from Nursery up to +2 level. Thousands of students of Spring Dale Public School and other institutions wouldn't need look beyond Global Knowledge Park for whatever courses or programme they wish to pursue to advance their career.
